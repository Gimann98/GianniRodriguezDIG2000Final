Games

Video games was one of the emerging technologies that we learned about during this section and I believe that they will help humanity. I may be biased based upon my personal liking of video games but I think that utilizing games in the right ways would boost moral and productivity. Imagine if when you go to school instead of using a textbook, pen, and paper you use a console and learn through an interactive video game. This would be a lot more entertaining and therefore keep people more engaged so that they may actaully learn something and grow smarter.

Deepfakes

This is something that I can only see doing harm to the world. Deepfakes are able to copy almost the exact look of someone and make them do anything that you would want them to. This could be used to blackmail someone, imcriminate someone, make someone look bad, etc. There are so many bad outcomes that can come out of deepfaking someone that I think that this technology needs to be regulated in someway before it gets out of hand.

VR/AR

VR and AR are things that I think would be insanely beneficial to humanity when they are implented more. VR/AR could be used in universities to have virtual labs so that it cuts back on lab costs and allows students to do things that may not have been able to before. Same goes for the real world, using VR in medical centers would be amazing, being able to practice a procedure or surgery in VR would be mounds better than on a real patient, there is no risk involved in VR.
